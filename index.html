01 · Project Overview

From signal noise to emotional clarity

Built to solve a real gap: support and engagement tools that are blind to how users actually feel.

Multimodal Emotional Intelligence System

Problem: Support and engagement platforms lose 34% of at-risk users because they can't detect emotional distress signals in real time — neither from faces nor language, let alone combined.

Fused CNN-based facial expression recognition with transformer-based text sentiment to produce a unified emotion embedding, risk score, and escalation flag — reducing missed escalations by an estimated 40%.

The Problem

Single-modal systems miss 60% of emotional signals

Facial analysis alone is fooled by poker faces. Text sentiment alone misses visual distress cues. Neither is sufficient. The gap costs businesses in churn, escalations, and missed care moments.

The Solution

Late-fusion multimodal architecture with explainability

Two encoder branches (CNN for vision, transformer for text) feed into a cross-modal attention fusion layer. The unified embedding powers emotion classification, risk scoring, and actionable recommendations.

Technical Approach

Pre-trained encoders + learned fusion head

Leverages FER2013-fine-tuned ResNet-50 for facial features and a RoBERTa sentiment backbone — fused via a lightweight attention mechanism. No novel architecture required; integration is the moat.

05 · Roadmap

Built in three phases

Designed to ship a working MVP fast, then layer in sophistication.

PHASE 01 — MVP ✓
Core Pipeline
  • Facial emotion detection (ResNet-50 on FER2013)
  • Text sentiment analysis (RoBERTa fine-tuned)
  • Late-fusion model (weighted concat + MLP)
  • FastAPI inference endpoint
  • Streamlit demo dashboard
PHASE 02 — ENHANCEMENT
Multimodal Reasoning
  • Cross-modal attention fusion (replace concat)
  • Emotion trend analytics over session time
  • Risk flagging and escalation triggers
  • Voice tone integration (whisper features)
  • Real-time streaming inference
PHASE 03 — RESPONSIBLE AI
Explainability & Fairness
  • SHAP per-prediction explanations
  • GradCAM facial region attribution
  • Demographic bias audit (AIF360)
  • Fairness-constrained fine-tuning
  • Model card & audit reports
